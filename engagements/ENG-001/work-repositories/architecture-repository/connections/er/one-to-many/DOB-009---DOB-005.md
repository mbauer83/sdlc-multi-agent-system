WorkflowState (DOB-009) holds a reference to at most one AlgedonicSignal (DOB-005) via its `last_algedonic: AlgedonicSignal | None` field. The cardinality is zero-or-one: a WorkflowState without any algedonic history has `last_algedonic = None`; once any algedonic signal has been raised, the field holds the most recent one. Historical algedonic signals are retrievable from the EventStore via `algedonic.raised` event records.
