## Architecture Board

A business collaboration comprising the SA Role (ACT-003), SwA Role (ACT-004), and User (ACT-001) acting jointly in enterprise governance capacity. The sole authority with write access to `enterprise-repository/`. Conducts enterprise promotion reviews, approves cross-engagement standards, and maintains the global ID counter registry. Operates outside the engagement workflow — it is an external collaboration from the engagement's perspective.
