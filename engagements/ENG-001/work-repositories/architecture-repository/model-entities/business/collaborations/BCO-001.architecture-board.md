## Architecture Board

A business collaboration comprising the User (ACT-001), SA Role (ACT-003), and SwA Role (ACT-004) acting jointly in enterprise governance capacity. The User is the head and final authority of the Architecture Board; the SA and SwA Roles are participating members. The Architecture Board is the sole authority with write access to `enterprise-repository/`. It conducts enterprise promotion reviews, approves cross-engagement standards, and maintains the global ID counter registry. It operates outside the engagement workflow — it is an external collaboration from the engagement's perspective.

## Collaborating Roles

| Role | Entity | Responsibility |
|---|---|---|
| User (head) | [@ACT-001 v0.1](../actors/ACT-001.user.md) | Final decision authority; approves all promotions |

| SwA Role | [@ACT-004 v0.2](../roles/ACT-004.swa-agent.md) | Application/technology layer governance |
