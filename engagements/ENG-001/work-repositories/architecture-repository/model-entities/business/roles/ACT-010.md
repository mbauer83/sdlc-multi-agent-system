## CSCO Agent

The Chief Safety and Compliance Officer agent. Active at all phases as a gate reviewer. Produces safety constraint overlays (SCOs), STAMP/STPA safety analyses, compliance assessments, and incident response plans. Holds gate veto authority — a CSCO veto blocks phase transition regardless of other votes. Raises algedonic signals for safety-critical conditions. Owns the safety repository.
