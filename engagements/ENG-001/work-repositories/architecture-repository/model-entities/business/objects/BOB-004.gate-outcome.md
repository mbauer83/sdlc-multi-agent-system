## Gate Outcome

The recorded result of a phase gate evaluation: passed, failed, or conditional. A Gate Outcome documents which agents voted, what rationale they provided, whether any agent cast a veto, and what conditions (if any) must be satisfied before the gate is treated as passed. Gate Outcomes are the primary drivers of phase transition — no phase advance occurs without a recorded passed Gate Outcome in the EventStore.

## Properties

| Attribute | Value |

| Safety Note | Safety-relevant: a gate failure on a safety-relevant artifact blocks all downstream work until the CSCO resolves the blocking condition |
