## Sprint Plan

The ordered sequence of specialist agent invocations for the current sprint, including the active phase, open CQs to resolve, outstanding handoffs to process, and gate state. Produced at the start of every sprint by Sprint Planning (BPR-001) and consumed immediately by Skill Execution (BPR-002) to drive the agent dispatch sequence. Persists for the duration of the sprint then superseded by the next plan.

## Properties

| Attribute | Value |
|---|---|
| Lifecycle | Created by BPR-001 (Sprint Planning); consumed by BPR-002 (Skill Execution); superseded at sprint close |
| VS Stage Coverage | Present in every VS-001 stage; each stage may span one or more sprints |
| Application Layer Counterpart | DOB-009 (WorkflowState) — captures current phase, sprint number, open CQs, handoff queue, gate outcomes |

## Concept Map Entry

The Sprint Plan at the business layer is the human-intelligible view of what the system has decided to do and in what order. DOB-009 (WorkflowState) is its machine representation inside the LangGraph graph state.
