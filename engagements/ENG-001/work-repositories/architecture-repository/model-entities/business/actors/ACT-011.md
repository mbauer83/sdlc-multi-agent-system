## Architecture Board

The enterprise governance body that reviews and approves promoted entities. The sole authority with write access to `enterprise-repository/`. Conducts enterprise promotion reviews, approves cross-engagement standards, and maintains the global ID counter registry. Operates outside the engagement workflow — it is an external actor from the engagement's perspective.
