## Architecture Board

The governance body responsible for enterprise-level architecture promotion decisions. Reviews promoted entities from engagement repositories, approves cross-engagement standards, and is the sole authority with write access to `enterprise-repository/`. Architecture Board members are not engagement participants — they act at the enterprise scope.
