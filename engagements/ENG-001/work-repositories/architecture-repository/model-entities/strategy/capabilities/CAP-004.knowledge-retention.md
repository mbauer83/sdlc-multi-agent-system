## Knowledge Retention

The ability to record, retrieve, and synthesise learnings across skills, sprints, and engagements. Implemented by the LearningStore (LangGraph BaseStore) with optional sqlite-vec semantic retrieval. Cross-agent learnings are periodically synthesised by the PM and made available in a shared index.
